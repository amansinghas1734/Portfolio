Bal.dev

Bal.dev stands for Blockchain Authentication Leisure Developers. It is a decentralized document verification platform built using blockchain technology. It aims to streamline and secure the process of validating documents by eliminating the need for centralized authorities. By leveraging smart contracts, Bal.dev ensures tamper-proof, transparent, and efficient authentication of records—ideal for developers seeking a lightweight yet robust verification system.

JetStart
JetStart is a web platform designed to connect job seekers with startups and emerging companies. It not only facilitates job opportunities but also encourages applicants to engage with companies on innovative business ideas. The platform aims to bridge the gap between talent and startup ventures, fostering a collaborative ecosystem for growth and innovation.
Thala

Thala is a unique business model that tokenizes social media influencers based on their reach and engagement metrics, drawing inspiration from the stock market. Each influencer is represented as a digital asset whose value fluctuates with their online impact, allowing users to "invest" in influencers much like stocks. The project explores the intersection of blockchain, social media, and market dynamics to create a novel influencer economy.

Smart Chair
Smart Chair is an innovative embedded system designed to improve user posture and health during prolonged sitting. It uses posture sensors to detect incorrect sitting positions and alerts the user with an alarm. Users can set a custom time interval, after which the chair emits a beep to encourage movement or posture correction. The system also integrates a heart rate sensor to monitor the user's pulse in real time. It aims to reduce the risk of musculoskeletal issues and sedentary lifestyle-related problems. Smart Chair is ideal for students, professionals, and anyone with long sitting hours.
